About WDR5

Information for families looking for a starting point after diagnosis.

What is WDR5?

WDR5 is a gene involved in important developmental processes. Changes in this gene have been linked with a rare neurodevelopmental disorder.

This page is family-led and should not replace medical advice. We aim to explain what we have learned in a way that is easier for families to understand.
